Welcome to Clouds Drawing

Purpose

My name is Mikayla, an I like to draw. To me drawing is fun and challenging. I designed this site for myself, mom, classmates, and anyone who wants to learn how to draw.

This is a site for children and young adults that are either new to drawing or looking to build on the skills they have. Here you can choose your prefered drawing style or challenge yourself in learning a new technique. All you have to do is type in a description of the image you want to draw to be provided with step by step instrucctions on how to draw your desired image in the drawing style of your choosing.
